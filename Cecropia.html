Overview


Cecropia Solutions began a redesign of itâ€™s entire main platform with the vision of becoming more known. With the redesign of its entire main platform, the company aims to create a more user-friendly and intuitive platform that will not only attract new clients but also potential employees. The first step in this process was to identify any usability issues that may exist within the current platform. By doing so we would be able to gain a better understanding of what changes needed to be made to improve the overall user experience.


This included conducting user testing and gathering feedback from existing clients and employees. With this information in hand we would be able to determine the critical features that needed to be prioritized for the next iteration of the platform. Overall, this project represented an important step forward. By investing in the redesign of its platform, the company demonstrates its commitment to providing a high-quality user experience for all who interact with its platform.
