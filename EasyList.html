EasyList Platform Creation

Overview


EasyList started out as a concept of a platform that would facilitate the exchange of requested services from businesses or entrepreneurs and would provide a way for users to freelance and offer their services for compensation of their chosing.


EasyList is a system destinated for recording product offers and requests created by users in real time. It's platform is designed to save businesses and users time and money in automating research of resources. It provides a safe and efficient way of getting the best services without the hastle of searching for someone and reviewing their profile.
